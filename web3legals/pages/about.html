About the Firm

Why Web3Legals Exists

When Rahul Pareek started advising crypto clients in the early days of blockchain adoption, he noticed a fundamental problem: the legal industry wasn't keeping pace with the technology. Founders were getting generic advice that didn't account for the unique nature of decentralised systems, token economics, or cross-border digital assets.

Web3Legals was founded to fix that gap. Rather than retrofitting traditional corporate law onto Web3 businesses, we built a practice from the ground up — one where legal counsel understands tokenomics, can read smart contracts, and knows how different regulatory regimes treat on-chain activity.

Today, Web3Legals serves clients across six continents — from solo founders launching their first NFT project to enterprise-grade crypto exchanges seeking multi-jurisdiction licensing. Our global regulatory network and deep Web3 fluency give clients an edge that traditional law firms simply cannot match.
